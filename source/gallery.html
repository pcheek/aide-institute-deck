08 · Methodology Pillar Explainer1200 × 1200
Methodologyv 2.1
The four pillars.
Two dimensions — Leadership (what executives know & say) and Company (what the org prioritizes & builds) — each split into two scoring pillars.
LITERACY
KNOW
ADVOCACY
SAY
ORIENTATION
PRIORITIZE
IMPLEMENTATION
BUILD
Literacy
What executives know.
Measured share of C-suite and board demonstrating AI literacy via earnings calls, public statements, and credentialed background.
Advocacy
What executives say.
Volume and substance of AI-related communication by executives on LinkedIn, in interviews, and at industry events.
Orientation
What the org prioritizes.
Strategic AI commitment across six channels — 10-Ks, hiring, capex, partnerships, governance, and stated strategy.
Implementation
What the org builds.
Tangible AI deployment across six channels — products shipped, customer-facing AI, internal tools, and measurable outcomes.
