Literacy
What executives know.
Measured share of C-suite and board demonstrating AI literacy via earnings calls, public statements, and credentialed background.
Advocacy
What executives say.
Volume and substance of AI-related communication on LinkedIn, in interviews, and at industry events.
Orientation
What the org prioritizes.
Strategic AI commitment across six channels — 10-Ks, hiring, capex, partnerships, governance, stated strategy.
Implementation
What the org builds.
Tangible AI deployment across six channels — products shipped, customer-facing AI, internal tools, measurable outcomes.
